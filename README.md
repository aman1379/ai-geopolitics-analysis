# ai-geopolitics-analysis
AI in Geopolitics

## What I'm Exploring
How AI is being used in surveillance, intelligence, and geopolitical decision-making.

## Key Questions
- Where can AI systems fail in high-stakes environments?
- How do different countries approach AI deployment?
- What are the risks of rapid, automated decision-making?

## Focus
- Military surveillance systems
- Bias and misclassification risks
- Global AI strategies (US, China, Europe)

## Status
Work in progress.**

## Initial Simulation Insight

A simple simulation was conducted to understand error rates in AI-based classification systems.

Key observations:
- False positives can incorrectly flag civilians as threats
- False negatives can fail to detect actual threats

Even with relatively high accuracy, these errors persist.

**This simulation is a simplified representation and not based on real-world classified data**

### Implication
In high-stakes environments like surveillance and military decision-making, small error rates can lead to significant real-world consequences, including escalation of conflict or failure in threat detection.
